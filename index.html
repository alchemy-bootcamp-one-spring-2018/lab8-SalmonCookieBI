Salmon Cookies

About Us

From the earliest days of the Northwest, people have been enjoying the benefits of eating Salmon. In more recent times, our area has become known for its delicious cookies. Finally, we have brought the two together!

Introducing Salmon Cookies - a nutritious and delicious treat combining the great tastes of salmon and freshly baked cookies into one unique Northwest delicacy.

Our bakers start early each morning, visiting the fish market to select only the sweetest and tastiest salmon from each day's catch. Then, the fish is prepared and finely-ground to be used as the base for our variety of cookie flavors - follow your nose to one of our always-busy locations today!
