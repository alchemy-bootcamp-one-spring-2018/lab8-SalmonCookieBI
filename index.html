Cookies. Redefined.

Welcome to Saumon's Cookies. We're a bakery in Portland with a local twist: all of our baked goods use salmon in them. We mix both sweet and savory cookies, muffins, croissants, and other assorted pastries with the meaty yet mild taste of salmon.

The local bounty of salmon in the Pacific Northwest region makes this marriage of sweet and savory a no-brainer. And, of course, we practice sustainability and eco-friendly policies in the making of our baked goods - it's the very least we can do.

Drop by one of our five locations in the Portland metro area and ask for a sample.

We think you're going to love it.
