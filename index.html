Meet Pat

Born to be an innovator with a boundless imagination and a passion for life, adventure, and cookies, Pat brings his secret recipe for salmon cookies to Portland at last.

Featured Cookie

Coho & Kale salmon cookies herald the coming of summer, with flavor profiles featuring vanilla and cinnamon. Our salmon cookies are sourced from farms that employ sustainable methods and use environmentally friendly practices. Gluten-free option available.

Featured Coffee

Our local roasters, Triple Crown Coffee, bring the beauty of coffee from farm to cup. They are dedicated to the craft of fine coffee and the preservation of our national scenic hiking trails. They specialize in roasting small batches, making each coffee unique in flavor and of the highest quality for your enjoyment.
